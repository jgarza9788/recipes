# ChickenSalad_Keto
rating: ?

## Note
this works for thighs 
but should work for breasts

## ingredients
* Chicken (cans of chicken)
* Mayo
* Celery
* Romain Lettuce 
* Salt, Peppper and Garlic Powder

## Steps
1. mix the ingredients
* 3 cans of chicken
* 1ish cup of Mayo
* 1ish cup of Celery
* 1.5 TP of salt
* 1.5 TP of pepper
* 1.5 TP of garlic powder
2. place on romain Lettuce leafs and eat like a taco
