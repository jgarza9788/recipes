# Red Chicken
or Chipotle Chicken


## ingredients
* Chicken Thighs
* Chipotle in adobo Sauce (2 cans)
* Onion

## tools
* blender   
* instapot  

## Steps
1. blend 2 cans of Chipotle in abobo Sauce
2. put Chickens with blend mix in instapot
3. put Chopped onion on the top
4. Pressure Cook for 20mins on High
