# Butternut Squash

## ingredients
* Butternut Squash
* Olive Oil
* Garlic
* Salt & Pepper


## Steps
1. PreHeat oven to 400 degrees
2. Peel, de-seed, and cut the Butternut Squash into 1 inch cubes
3. coat squash in olive oil and garlic (salt and pepper to taste)
4. place on baking sheet for 25-30 mins
