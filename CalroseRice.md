# Calrose Rice
rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

## ingredients
* Calrose Rice
* rice cooker
* water
* pineapple (optional)
* hot links (optional)



## Steps
1. Rinsing (only if non-enriched)
- put 2 cups of rice in a pot with water.
- stir
- drain the water out
- repeat until water is clear

2. Soaking


3. Cook
- use white rice setting
